Greg Bee


Greg Bee is a traveling poet, storyteller and motivational speaker. He is currently based in Eureka, California, where he is a registered nurse, mental health advocate and community activist. He has overcome bipolar disorder and substance use problems. His work deals with gender and sexuality, as well as social and political issues.

Book

Saying Goodbye to Toxic Boys

Novel

Saying Goodbye to Toxic Boys is a codependent love story. Maggie and Devin cling onto one another as trauma unfolds in both their lives. Together they learn if love and mindfulness can be enough to break their cycle.
