3542 Berry Street · Cheyenne Wells, CO 80810 · (317) 585-8468 · GregBeePoetry@gmail.com

Greg Bee is a traveling poet, story teller, and performer. His work deals with topics like mental health and addiction recovery, gender and sexuality, as well as social and political issues.

Book

Saying Goodbye to Toxic Boys

Novel

Saying Goodbye to Toxic Boys is a codependent love story. Maggie and Devin cling onto one another as trauma unfolds in both their lives. Together they learn if love and mindfulness can be enough to break their cycle.

12/01/19

Being Sad then Feeling Better

Poetry

Being Sad then Feeling Better is a 40 page chapbook with a collection of poetry from Greg Bee's early career.

Part 1 is poetry about depression and anxiety and Part 2 is poetry about hope and recovery.

Apart from being a web developer, I enjoy most of my time being outdoors. In the winter, I am an avid skier and novice ice climber. During the warmer months here in Colorado, I enjoy mountain biking, free climbing, and kayaking.

When forced indoors, I follow a number of sci-fi and fantasy genre movies and television shows, I am an aspiring chef, and I spend a large amount of my free time exploring the latest technology advancements in the front-end web development world.
